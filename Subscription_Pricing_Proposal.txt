# School Portal: Subscription Pricing Proposal (NGN)

Based on the feature structure implemented in the Abecedarian Portal, the application is divided into three distinct subscription tiers. Below is a strategic breakdown of what each tier offers, target demographics, and recommended pricing models tailored for the Nigerian educational software market.

## Feature Breakdown by Plan

### 1. Basic Plan (The Foundation)
**Target:** Small or budget-conscious schools needing to digitize core records.
**Features Included:**
*   **Student Management:** Enrollments, updates, and class assignments.
*   **Staff Management:** Basic user accounts (Teachers, Head Teachers, Principals).
*   **Academics & Grading:** Subject assignment, score entry (CA & Exam), and Nigerian Grading System (A1-F9).
*   **Result Generation:** Standard report card generation (PDFs).
*   **Basic Dashboards:** General overviews for all user roles.

### 2. Standard Plan (The Academic Suite)
**Target:** Growing schools that want strict control over curriculum delivery and teacher accountability.
**Features Included:**
*   *Everything in the Basic Plan.*
*   **Lesson Plan Module:** Teachers can create, submit, and generate PDFs of lesson plans.
*   **Lesson Plan Approval:** Admins and Principals can review, approve, or reject lesson plans with feedback notes.

### 3. Deluxe Plan (The Enterprise Suite)
**Target:** Established, medium-to-large schools requiring strict financial oversight, workflow approvals, and auditing.
**Features Included:**
*   *Everything in the Standard Plan.*
*   **Advanced Finance Module:** Fee structures, bulk bill generation, expense tracking, income/expense reports, debtor management, and automated balance reminders.
*   **Payment Processing:** Recording payments, issuing credit notes, reversing payments, and generating receipt PDFs.
*   **Maker-Checker Workflow (Pending Tasks):** Requires Admin approval for critical actions (e.g., student deletions, major grading changes, or financial adjustments performed by Admin Assistants).
*   **System Audit Logs:** Detailed tracking of "who did what and when" across the entire portal.

## Recommended Pricing Models

In Nigeria, School Management Systems typically charge using one of two models: **Per-Student (Termly)** or a **Flat Termly/Annual Fee**. 

> [!TIP]
> **Recommendation:** The **Flat Fee model with student tiers** (e.g., up to 300 students, up to 500, etc.) is often preferred by Nigerian school owners as it provides predictable costs, while the Per-Student model is better for maximizing your revenue from very large schools.

### Option A: Flat Termly Fee (Recommended for Small-Medium Schools)
*Assuming a standard school size of 100 - 300 students.*

| Plan | Suggested Price (Per Term) | Suggested Price (Per Year / 3 Terms) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Plan** | ₦30,000 - ₦50,000 | ₦80,000 - ₦135,000 *(10% discount)* |
| **Standard Plan** | ₦60,000 - ₦85,000 | ₦160,000 - ₦230,000 *(10% discount)*|
| **Deluxe Plan** | ₦100,000 - ₦150,000 | ₦270,000 - ₦400,000 *(10% discount)* |

*Note: For schools with over 500 students, you can charge a 50% premium on the flat fee.*

### Option B: Per-Student Fee (Recommended for Large Schools)
*Billed at the beginning of each term based on active student enrollment.*

| Plan | Suggested Price (Per Student / Term) | Minimum Charge (Per Term) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Plan** | ₦300 - ₦500 | ₦25,000 |
| **Standard Plan** | ₦700 - ₦1,000 | ₦50,000 |
| **Deluxe Plan** | ₦1,500 - ₦2,000 | ₦100,000 |

## Strategy & Upselling

1. **The "Basic" Hook:** Keep the Basic plan affordable. Once a school inputs all their students and staff into your system, they are highly unlikely to leave.
2. **The "Standard" Push:** Sell the Standard plan by highlighting *Teacher Accountability*. School owners love ensuring teachers are doing their jobs; the Lesson Plan approval workflow is a major selling point.
3. **The "Deluxe" Necessity:** Once a school trusts your software with academics, they will naturally want to track school fees. At that point, the jump to the Deluxe plan becomes a no-brainer because it stops financial leakages and tracks every naira.
